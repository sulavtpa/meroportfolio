About My Journey

I am Sulav Thapa, a young programmer in my early 20s, fascinated by this mix of logic and creativity. The journey for me has begun with an interest in numbers, logic, and electrical systems, which made it only logical to proceed to programming. I am eager about C/C++, Algorithms, and Systems-level programming, immersing myself in the details of how things work under the hood. I have considerable exposure to JavaScript, Java, and web development, with an insatiable appetite to learn anything that comes along.

Beyond the code, you can find me playing chess, absorbed in a good read, and chasing new ideas. For me, learning and coding are not tasks but exciting discoveries. I am passionate about coding with elegance and efficiency and learning the "why" for every "how".
